Introduction

League of Legends is a Free to Play (FTP) Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA). The goal of the game is to work with 4 teammates to destroy your opponents Nexus or base. There are over 150 champions to choose from and each champion has 4 unique abilities.
